SELENA GOMEZ

18 Apr 2019

Hello bright things,

Here brings another blog post of a recent shoot, I’m so behind in updating my work online so I have a few more shoots to post in the next coming weeks.

This set of images were taken for the talented and sweet, Simreteab whom is a musician originally from Perth, living in Sydney Australia. On the day of the shoot we’d planned to meet up and spend the afternoon shooting on suburban streets, lighting candles and burning sage sticks. At first it was pouring with rain, so we set off to have some hot beverages indoors + once it cleared up we decided to go back to the desired location + shoot in the clouded but warm light.

Below are the images from the day! Enjoy and be sure to listen to her music here, she’s definitely my new favourite.

-Simreteab. xx
